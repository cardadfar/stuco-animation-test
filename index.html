98-331: Student-Taught Course | Animation & Video Editing

Fall 2018

Thurs. 6:30 - 8:00pm | CFA 317

3 Units

In this course, we will introduce studio standards for making videos and animations by merging classing animation techniques with professional video-editing techniques and software. Students will be exposed to Hand-Drawn Animation, Vector-Based Animation, Video Editing, Kinetic Typography & Motion Graphics. This course covers the entire creative process from storyboarding & animatics to editing & post-production. No artistic background is required, although some may find the animation portion easier with drawing experience.
