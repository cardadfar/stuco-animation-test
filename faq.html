What experience is required to take the course?

No prior knowledge of art is assumed. Students with drawing experience will find the Animation unit easier. Students without drawing experience will use the Animation unit as means of gaining drawing experience.

I don't have access to any of the Adobe software on my laptop?

CFA rooms 317 & 318 come loaded with the Adobe Creative Suite. Adobe also offers 7-day free trials of all their software. For those who absolutely need Adobe software locally, Adobe finances all their software for a flat montly rate that is discouted for students.

I am skilled already in [insert name of non-adobe software here]. Can I do my work in this software?

For the most part, yes. The course will still be taught with the Adobe Creative Suite. Some units are more specific to a certain Adobe software, but unless otherwise specified, you are free to use any software.

How are assignments graded?

Assignments are graded based on completion. The objective of an assignment is to demonstrate a proper understanding of last week's topics. It should be evident that you spent at least 30 minutes working on the assignment.

Am I allowed to use copyrighted audio/visuals in my assignments?

Yes. Be sure to give credit where credit is due.

Are there late days for assignments?

I won't be a stickler about due dates. It is in your best interest to complete the weekly assignments in a timely manner so that your work doesn't pile up. I will be a bit more stict about deadlines as the Midterm & Final approaches.

I know I won't be in class on a given day. Should I let you know?

Don't worry about emailing me. You can look at the syllabus to see what we will cover that day and use YouTube, Lynda, & other resources to research the topic so that you can complete the weekly 'quick-edit' on your own.

I am really busy this week and cannot complete my assignment. What should I do?

A StuCo should be the last of your worries. Take a late-day, or a late-week. You are allowed to skip 2 assignments (not including the Midterm & Final).

When are your office hours?

Office hours are by email appointment within 24 hours notice.
